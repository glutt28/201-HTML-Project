About Us

Our Founder

Michael Mathew — or simply Mathew to friends and regulars — is the passionate mind behind this coffee haven. With a solid background in IT engineering and a long list of achievements in tech certifications, education mentorship, math olympiads, and martial arts, Mathew brings a rare mix of structure and soul into everything he builds.

An alumnus of the Sumatera Institute of Technology, Mathew spent much of his student life nestled in coffee shop corners — learning, coding, reading, and reflecting with an Americano in hand. To him, a coffee shop has always been more than just a place to grab a drink; it’s a space for focus, stillness, and inspiration.

Driven by a dream to create a space that feels like home, Mathew built this café not just to serve great coffee, but to offer a warm, welcoming atmosphere where people can pause, breathe, and just be. Coming from a humble family background, the journey here was far from easy — but it’s one he pursued with heart and perseverance.

When he’s not working behind the scenes, you might find Mathew strumming a guitar, practicing Pencak Silat or boxing, swimming, cycling, or simply jogging at sunrise. And, of course, always coding — still a passion close to his heart.

“Hey — welcome in. Whether you’re chasing a deadline, catching your breath, or just curious to try something new, this space was made for you. Grab a cup, take a seat, and stay as long as you like. We’re happy you’re here.” - Mathew -

Our .Coffee

Welcome to .Coffee — your third home after family and best friends. Tucked away near the Sumatera Institute of Technology in Lampung, .Coffee blends comfort, calm, and creativity into one cozy space. With our tagline “Fuel your Flow, Feel at Home,” we’re more than just a coffee shop — we’re your go-to corner for focus, reflection, and unwinding.

Inside, you’ll find a serene, library-like indoor space perfect for studying or deep work, while our outdoor area offers a chill, open-air vibe for conversations, live music, or just letting your thoughts breathe. Whether you're lounging on a sofa, flipping through a self-development or IT book from our mini library, or enjoying a board game with friends, there's always a spot at .Coffee that feels made just for you.

Our menu features hand-selected Arabica and Robusta beans, served in a variety of styles — but our customizable Americano and Affogato stand out as local favorites. Not into coffee? We’ve got you covered with milk-based drinks, fresh fruit-infused options, comforting meals, and baked goods.

With reliable WiFi, charging spots, and a thoughtful layout including high tables, sofas, and quiet corners, .Coffee is built for both productivity and peace. Whether you’re here to focus or just feel at home, we’re always glad you came.
